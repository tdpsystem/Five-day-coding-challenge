How to make a cup of tea

At the very least tea is a flavourful way of getting the necessary fluids you need on a regular basis. On top of that , studies show that teas can help protect your teeth and heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Water
  • Milk - Dairy/Plant based (Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

Preparation " aria-hidden="true">

  1. Run the tap a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in the water helps flavour.
  2. Pop a tea bag into your mug (always a big mug).
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly.
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all of its flavour, so give it 3-4 mins to do its thing. This is the perfect time to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about holiday.
  5. Before removing the tea bag gently squeeze it against the side of the cup with a spoon not too much though otherwise you will make it bitter
  6. Once you have removed the tea bag you can add milk or sugar/honey if you dresire or you can drink it plain
  7. Enjoy!
